# Bodhi of the Falling Leaves

**Bodhi NPC - Standalone Version for BG2EE & EET**

---

**Project Address:** https://github.com/trow/BodhiNPC

This mod extracts Bodhi from the All Things Mazzy (ATM) mod, making her available as a standalone NPC companion for those who wish to experience Bodhi's content without installing the full ATM package.

> This project is based on the Bodhi-related content originally created for All Things Mazzy. It has been extracted and released as a standalone mod with the kind [permission](https://forums.beamdog.com/discussion/71473/_/p46) of the ATM authors. No content has been removed or altered from the original Bodhi component—this is purely a standalone release for players who want Bodhi without the full ATM installation.

## Project Overview

This mod transforms Bodhi into a fully functional NPC companion, available throughout Shadows of Amn and Throne of Bhaal. She comes with banters, dialogues, interjections, character sounds, and epilogues.

### Bodhi NPC (SoA & ToB)

Bodhi joins as a fully functional companion with:
- Banters with various party members, including Korgan, Clara, and Edwin
- Two unique Bodhi-Hexxat dialogues
- Numerous interjections throughout the game
- Custom character sounds
- Multiple epilogue variations based on your choices

**Availability:**
- SoA content becomes available if you worked for Bodhi during Chapter 3
- ToB content continues her story into the expansion

**Compatibility Notes:**
- All Good-aligned vanilla recruitable party members are incompatible with Bodhi and will object to her presence in your party
- Bodhi is the enemy of vanilla Drizzt (but likely not Region of Terror Drizzt) and the Order of the Radiant Heart
- Fully compatible with Ascension Mod—the game registers this version of Bodhi and Ascension Bodhi as two different characters

### Bodhi-the-Elf NPC (ToB Only)

At the end of Shadows of Amn, should you direct Bodhi to accept Queen Ellesime's offer, Bodhi-the-Elf will ask to join your party in Throne of Bhaal.

**Bodhi-the-Elf Details:**
- Chaotic Evil Elven Monk
- A relatively quiet Easter-egg NPC, similar to Wilson NPC in BG2EE
- Good-aligned vanilla recruitable NPCs will travel with her, with the exception of:
  - Mazzy
  - Anomen (if he's still with the Order)
  - Keldorn

## Note on Installation

This is a standalone version of Bodhi from All Things Mazzy. If you already have ATM installed, you do not need this mod—the Bodhi content is already included. This release is specifically for players who wish to install Bodhi without the full ATM package.

## Compatibility, Installation & Known Issues

**Compatibility Notes:**
- Compatible with most mods
- **Do NOT install alongside All Things Mazzy** (this mod contains duplicate content from ATM's Bodhi component)
- Good-aligned vanilla NPCs (Aerie, Jaheira, Minsc, etc.) will not travel with Bodhi
- Drizzt encounters may conflict—Bodhi is programmed as Drizzt's enemy

**Installation Order:**
- Install after Living Clara mod for optimal crossmod interactions
- On EET installations: install before EET_End

**Known Issues:**
- None reported at this time. Please report any bugs through the project's GitHub issues page.
